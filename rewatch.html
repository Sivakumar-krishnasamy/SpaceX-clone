February 18, 2025

Starlink Mission

On Tuesday, February 18 at 6:21 p.m. ET, Falcon 9 launched 23 Starlink satellites to low-Earth
orbit from Space Launch Complex 40 (SLC-40) at Cape Canaveral Space Force Station in
Florida.

This was the 16th flight for the first stage booster supporting this mission, which previously
launched Ax-2, Euclid, Ax-3, CRS-30, Astra 1P, NG-21, and now 10 Starlink missions. Following
stage separation, the first stage landed on the Just Read the Instructions droneship,
completing our first landing off the coast of The Bahamas.
